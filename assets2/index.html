CAMIGUIN


Camiguin, situated in the heart of the Bohol Sea and located 100 kilometers north of Cagayan de Oro City in Misamis Oriental, is an island province. Despite its small size, spanning only 92 square miles, Camiguin boasts numerous tourist attractions waiting to be explored. This island paradise is adorned with stunning beaches, majestic waterfalls, invigorating hot springs, refreshing cold springs, and the most delectable Lanzones fruit found in the Philippines.

White Island



Book Now 15% OFF

White Island, a paradise for beach enthusiasts, boasts white sand, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant underwater scenery. As a sandbar, its shape fluctuates with the tides and currents, making it a dynamic destination year-round. However, it disappears during high tide, so consulting locals for tide schedules before renting a boat is advisable.

Mantigue Island



Book Now 25% OFF

In contrast to White Island's sandbar, Mantigue Island offers a picturesque blend of a small forest enclave enveloped by white sandy shores and turquoise waters, presenting a stunning natural vista. However, its true allure lies beneath the surface. Nestled within a protected marine sanctuary, Mantigue Island prohibits fishing activities, fostering a flourishing marine ecosystem. Spanning a modest 4 acres, this island is a haven for snorkelers and divers seeking vibrant underwater experiences. Explore Mantigue Island's depths to behold its captivating coral gardens and diverse marine life.
